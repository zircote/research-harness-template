## Headline

Dated 28 June 2026, covering the window since OKF v0.1 shipped on 12 June 2026. Verdict on the layering decision is GO on the evidence: MIF can serve as the typed-modeling, provenance, and temporal spine beneath OKF's git-distributable markdown packaging. The layering is technically feasible through OKF's own extension seam, materially differentiated from prior art, and aimed at a real and growing market. The single open risk is adoption, not capability.

## What's New

- OKF v0.1 shipped on 12 June 2026 and deliberately leaves the spine empty. Google Cloud's vendor-neutral markdown-directory spec requires only a `type` field; it formalizes the LLM-wiki pattern but intentionally omits formal ontology, typed inter-concept links (plain untyped markdown hyperlinks only), and machine-readable provenance (only an optional `log.md` and a `#Citations` prose section). Early traction is real — 5,440 stars and 416 forks within weeks — but it is brand-new and pre-community-governed.
- MIF already supplies exactly that missing spine, and is the more mature half. MIF reached v1.0.0 (Released, stabilized 18 June 2026) and has been public since roughly February 2026, so it predates OKF v0.1; its constraint is distribution and adoption, not spec maturity. It contributes a first-class PROV-O-compatible provenance block (typed source, 0 to 1 confidence, agent identity), bi-temporal tracking with configurable decay versus OKF's single last-changed timestamp, and a formal ontology and entity-typing layer. It also adds typed, directed, strength-weighted relationship edges where OKF has only untyped prose links. Weakened caveat: the specific "nine structural-core types" enumeration is overstated (only four — relates-to, derived-from, supersedes, part-of — are MIF-core; the others are namespaced or custom), but the typed-edge advantage itself holds.
- The layering is mechanically clean and the differentiation survives scrutiny. OKF's permissive extension rule (producers MAY add frontmatter keys; consumers MUST preserve unknown keys) is the exact seam to inject MIF fields; the one tension is OKF's permissive-consumer model versus MIF's fail-closed validation. Against the prior art — Frictionless Data, JSON-LD and YAML-LD, schema.org, SKOS, PROV-O, RDF/OWL, and the Obsidian/Logseq/Roam PKM tools — none combines git-native authoring accessibility with typed, provenanced, and temporal depth. The Semantic Web's adoption failure and the consolidating RDF-star/PROV-O standards layer both argue for OKF+MIF's bet on pragmatic minimalism plus optional depth.
- Demand and market are favorable, with explicit statistical caveats. Hybrid graph-plus-vector (GraphRAG) is becoming the production default, agent memory's hardest unsolved problems are exactly staleness and provenance, and git-native markdown KM (Karpathy's LLM-wiki, Obsidian's 1.5M-plus users) is the practitioner trend OKF rides. The combined KM and enterprise-knowledge-graph TAM is large (about $25B in 2025). Weakened caveats — four market statistics that travel with the claim: the Gartner "80% of apps embed an AI agent by Q1 2026" figure is overstated against Gartner's own ~40%-by-end-2026 prediction; the "76% SaaS in 2025" a16z attribution could not be verified; and two AI-KM market-sizing growth rates ($7.71B at 47.2%; $4.2B to $36.5B) are soft, misattributed, and do not reconcile. The directional surges are real; the precise magnitudes are not load-bearing.

## Why It Matters

- The gap is the product. OKF's deliberate omissions are precisely the surface MIF fills, so the layered spine is a real, non-overlapping value-add rather than a reinvention.
- No spec-maturity risk on the MIF side. Because MIF is already at v1.0.0 RC and predates OKF, the modeling, provenance, and temporal layer is buildable today; the remaining work is integration and adoption, not standards invention.
- Defensible positioning. The clean feasibility seam plus the differentiation story put OKF+MIF in the unclaimed "structured but accessible" niche between thin PKM wikis and heavyweight graph databases.
- A funded buyer trajectory, to be read directionally. The pains (institutional-memory loss, LLM grounding, provenance tracking) are quantified and demand is rising, but pricing and TAM should be sized off the verified directional trend, not the four weakened headline figures. The decisive residual risk is OKF nascency: it has no ecosystem yet, and its minimalism may itself be the buyer preference.

## What's Next / Asks

- Decision: proceed with OKF as the packaging layer and MIF as the modeling, provenance, and temporal spine. Both thesis sub-questions — OKF's minimalism, and MIF's typed-relationship, provenance, and temporal advantage — are each backed by surviving findings.
- Owner, Spec/Eng: prototype the OKF-frontmatter-to-MIF-extension injection and resolve the permissive-consumer versus fail-closed-validation tension (define what a non-MIF-aware OKF consumer does when it encounters MIF fields).
- Owner, Strategy/GTM: validate the open-core model (free format, paid governance, hosting, and audit) against the verified directional demand; do not commit forecasts to the four weakened market statistics until they are re-sourced.
- Key risk to watch: OKF's minimalism may itself be the buyer preference, so MIF's richer layer must be opt-in and zero-friction or it is a solution without a segment. Input needed: at least one design partner that explicitly wants typed provenance.
- MIF's own gap is distribution and adoption — no large independent adopter base and governance still forming — so fund ecosystem and adoption work, not more spec work.
